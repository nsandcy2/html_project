Movie Overview

Interstellar is a 2014 epic science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. It stars Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain, and Michael Caine.

Release Date: November 7, 2014 (Worldwide), November 7, 2014 (India)

Music Director: Hans Zimmer

Movie Description

Interstellar follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet for humanity. It explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the survival of humanity.

Plot Summary

  1. Earth faces a global crop blight and a second Dust Bowl, making it increasingly uninhabitable.
  2. Former NASA pilot Cooper is recruited to lead a space mission to find a new home for humanity.
  3. Cooper and his team travel through a wormhole near Saturn to explore potential planets.
  4. They encounter time dilation and the relativity of time, leading to emotional and physical challenges.
  5. Cooper sacrifices himself to ensure the survival of his crew and the success of the mission.

Trivia

Did you know?

  • Interstellar's visual effects team studied the physics of black holes and consulted astrophysicist Kip Thorne to ensure scientific accuracy.
  • Matthew McConaughey's emotional performance in the film moved many audience members to tears.

Awards Won

  • Academy Awards (Oscars): Best Visual Effects (2015)
  • BAFTA Awards: Best Special Visual Effects (2015)
  • Saturn Awards: Best Science Fiction Film (2015)
